The opportunity

A strong business can still look invisible on paper.

Confirma is for entrepreneurs with real customers, real stock movement, and real cash flow, but without the formal reporting systems most lenders expect. The product creates a better path: record what already happened, confirm it clearly, and build a history over time.

For entrepreneurs

Keep the workflow close to the rhythm of trade.

  • Record sales and expenses in plain language.
  • Use a low-friction flow that works on familiar phones.
  • Build continuity instead of scrambling for retroactive paperwork.
For institutions

Read activity as operating signal, not just self-report.

Confirma helps lenders see ongoing business behavior in a clearer structure. That means less dependence on informal retellings and a stronger foundation for underwriting conversations.

The site leads with entrepreneurs because trust begins where the record is formed, but the eventual payoff includes banks, microfinance institutions, SACCOs, and development finance partners.
How it works

Say it. Review it. Confirm it. Keep it.

The product story is simple on purpose. Confirma reduces the distance between economic activity and durable evidence by making the confirmation moment the center of the system.

1

Capture

An entrepreneur speaks or types a transaction the way they naturally describe it.

2

Interpret

The system turns the input into a plain-language summary that can be checked immediately.

3

Commit

Only the confirmed version is written to the sequence and linked to the record before it.

Consistency becomes signal

Useful trust is earned through repeated confirmation, not polished storytelling.

Over time, the value of the ledger comes from persistence. Confirma is not trying to turn entrepreneurs into bookkeepers. It is helping them form evidence of business continuity in a way that can accumulate meaning month by month.

30 days Early continuity

The first month starts to show that recording is becoming a business habit rather than a one-time effort.

90 days Operational picture

Repeated activity begins to look more like a usable operating pattern instead of isolated entries.

180 days Deeper signal

Longer continuity creates a stronger basis for institution-facing review, reporting, and trust.
