For institutions

A clearer operating picture for businesses formal paperwork often misses.

Confirma gives institutions a more structured way to read business activity generated outside conventional bookkeeping systems. Instead of depending on retroactive summaries alone, lenders and partners can review records formed through user confirmation and preserved as a durable sequence.

Institution value

Not just more data. Better formed evidence.

The purpose of Confirma is not to flood lenders with unstructured activity. It is to create records that carry a more understandable trust posture because of how they were created, confirmed, and linked.

Problem

Spreadsheets arrive late

Many small businesses only reconstruct records when they are already in a financing process, which limits confidence.

Difference

Confirmation is part of formation

Confirma builds the review step into the system so that the user has a final checkpoint before the record becomes durable.

Outcome

Continuity becomes visible

Repeated records over time can offer a stronger operating picture than isolated documents collected at the edge of underwriting.

Why this matters

Confirma is more useful than self-reported summaries because the record-formation process is visible.

Institutions often face a tradeoff between excluding businesses with weak documentation and accepting noisy evidence. Confirma aims to make that tradeoff less severe by improving how records are formed before they ever reach the institution.

Reviewability

Records are easier to interpret.

  • Plain-language entries stay legible.
  • Confirmation makes the authority path easier to explain.
  • Append-only structure supports stronger continuity claims.
Practical effect

Institutions can focus on activity patterns, not just document polish.

The system does not replace credit policy. It improves one important ingredient: the quality and consistency of the raw operating picture being reviewed.

Use cases

Useful across lenders, microfinance, SACCOs, and development finance programs.

The product is especially relevant where businesses are economically active but do not yet fit cleanly inside formal documentation expectations.

Microfinance

Evaluate smaller businesses with more operating context.

Confirma can help surface continuity and transaction behavior where formal statements are not yet mature.

Banks

Add a stronger field-level view into underserved segments.

The record can complement relationship banking and ground underwriting in a more structured history.

Programs

Improve reporting pathways for targeted finance initiatives.

Development finance and ecosystem programs can use clearer transaction history to better understand business traction.

Verification direction

The sequence matters, not just the balance.

Confirma is built around the idea that reports should eventually carry chain-aware integrity clues, giving receiving institutions more confidence that the history was not silently altered after formation.

Patent position

The IP story supports the product story, not the other way around.

The filed provisional patent is a signal that the architecture is novel, but the main trust case for institutions remains the mechanics of confirmation, continuity, and reviewability.

Next step

Understand the product from the record-formation side first.

The clearest way to evaluate Confirma is to start with how records are created, confirmed, and preserved before they are ever exported.
