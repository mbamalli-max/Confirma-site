About Confirma

Building a record layer that respects the entrepreneur first.

Confirma exists because too many businesses create real value long before formal systems recognize them. The company is building a product that turns everyday activity into a clearer financial history without forcing users to become accountants first.

Get the App See the roadmap
What the company is trying to change

Formal trust should not begin only when paperwork appears.

Confirma is focused on the gap between economic activity and formal recognition. The product vision is to help overlooked businesses form records that are clear enough to travel into lending, reporting, and verification workflows without asking entrepreneurs to retrofit their lives into office-centric systems.

The brand stays product-led because the strongest story the company can tell is the one the system makes possible.
Mission

Make reliable financial history easier to form in the environments where people actually trade.

Confirma is designed for entrepreneurs working in markets, informal retail, transport, food, and other active sectors where value moves every day but formal records are often thin, fragmented, or delayed.

Principle one

Consent before authority

The platform centers the confirmation moment so that the user remains the final checkpoint before a record becomes durable.

Principle two

Evidence over reconstruction

The goal is to capture activity close to when it happens, instead of asking people to reconstruct months of work after the fact.

Patent

Protected architecture, kept in service of a practical mission.

Confirma's system design includes a filed USPTO provisional application covering the confirmation-gated authority architecture behind the product. The patent signal matters, but it is secondary to the usefulness and clarity of the product itself.

Filed

USPTO Provisional Patent Application No. 63/987,858

The filing reflects the novelty of the confirmation-centered ledger authority model and the trust mechanics it enables in constrained environments.

Interpretation

Innovation is useful only if the interaction stays understandable.

Confirma keeps the system legible: users should understand what they are confirming, and institutions should understand what kind of record they are reviewing.

Roadmap

Build the record well first, then deepen verification.

The roadmap is intentionally compact. Confirma is prioritizing the sequence of capabilities that make the ledger useful before expanding the surrounding institution-facing workflow.

Current foundation

Formation and continuity

  • Offline-friendly ledger workflow
  • Voice and text transaction capture
  • Confirmation-centered record creation
  • Dashboard and export primitives
Next layer

Stronger institution-facing trust

  • More formal verification outputs
  • Signature and validation pathways
  • Improved reporting and sharing workflows
  • Richer institution review surfaces
