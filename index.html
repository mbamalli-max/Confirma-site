Plain-language product story

Not accounting software. Record formation infrastructure.

Confirma is designed for people whose businesses are real, active, and valuable, but whose financial lives are still invisible to formal lenders.

🧺

For entrepreneurs

Market traders, food vendors, artisans, transport operators, and side hustlers can build usable financial history from daily activity they already understand.

🏦

For institutions

Banks, SACCOs, microfinance lenders, and development finance partners get transaction history that is structured, tamper-resistant, and easier to verify.

🔐

For trust

Every confirmed transaction becomes permanent, receives a unique ID, and links back to the previous record through a cryptographic hash chain.

How it works

Say it. Confirm it. Keep it.

Speak or type a transaction

“Sold rice for 15,000.” Voice-first on Android and Chrome, with text fallback everywhere else.

Review the summary

The app reads it back in plain language so the user stays in control before anything becomes final.

Confirm to write permanently

Only explicit confirmation creates a ledger record. No edits, no deletes, no admin override path.

Why it matters

Your activity is already a credit story.

Confirma helps record that story in a way lenders can take seriously. Over time, each confirmed entry contributes to a persistent financial trail that can support underwriting, reporting, and trust.

  • Trust tiers move from NEW to BRONZE, SILVER, and GOLD based on sustained activity.
  • Exports include a ledger root hash and integrity code for offline verification.
  • The product supports Nigeria and the United States, with multiple languages and accessible interaction modes.
Trust tiers

Consistency becomes signal.

Confirma rewards continuity, not hype. The more consistently a business records real activity, the stronger its financial identity becomes.

NEW Starting the record

Every ledger begins with first use. The goal is simple: create a clear, trustworthy habit of confirmation-based recording.

BRONZE • 30 days Early continuity

After a month of active history, the ledger starts showing usable persistence and a foundation for first-level review.

SILVER • 90 days Bank-relevant history

Ninety days starts to resemble a meaningful operating picture. This is where reporting and underwriting conversations become stronger.

GOLD • 180 days Established financial identity

Six months of consistent, append-only activity creates a deeper record that institutions can inspect with greater confidence.

“Your business activity is your credit history, if it’s recorded right.” Confirma is built around that idea from the first transaction to the exported report.
