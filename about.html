Mission, vision, and build status

Confirma is building financial identity infrastructure for overlooked businesses.

The mission is to unlock access to formal financial services for underserved populations, including unbanked entrepreneurs and persons with disabilities, by turning daily economic activity into verifiable, unrewritable history.

Version

Confirma v1.0.0

Phase 1: Local Immutable Foundation

Mission

Accessible records that can unlock formal trust.

Confirma is designed to convert daily economic activity into bank-verifiable financial identity through accessible, multi-modal interaction. The product meets users where they are instead of assuming formal systems already include them.

Vision

A global trust layer for informal participation.

Starting with Nigeria’s informal economy and designed for global applicability, Confirma aims to provide device-bound financial records and verified reports that unlock credit, capital, and institutional trust.

Intellectual property

USPTO Provisional Patent Application filed.

Application No: 63/987,858

Title: Confirmation-Gated Authority Architecture for Deterministic Formation of Immutable Ledger Records in Constrained Environments

This filing protects the core architecture behind how Confirma forms permanent records in environments where connectivity, literacy, and device conditions may be constrained.

Build status

What exists now and what comes next.

Phase 1 complete

Android PWA, voice and text input, immutable ledger, free export, dashboard chart, trust tier system, onboarding, settings, multilingual UI, Nigeria and USA support.

Phase 2 next

Device-bound Ed25519 signatures, server sync, fork detection, verified reports, and a live verification portal.

Phase 3

Bank API integration, loan-readiness notifications, agent verification workflow, and an iOS native app.

Long-term ambition

Confirma becomes a reusable trust layer wherever people have real economic participation but insufficient formal records.
