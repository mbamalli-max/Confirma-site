Your activity is already a credit story.

Confirma helps record that story in a way lenders can take seriously. Each confirmed entry builds a persistent financial trail that supports underwriting, reporting, and trust.

  • Trust tiers move from NEW to BRONZE, SILVER, and GOLD based on sustained activity.
  • Exports include a ledger root hash and integrity code for offline verification.
  • Supports Nigeria and the United States with multiple languages and input modes.
