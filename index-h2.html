Core infrastructure

Four pillars of verified trust

Every feature is designed around one constraint: the person confirming may be offline, working by voice, and unfamiliar with financial software.

Voice verification

Speak to confirm. Works offline, in any language. No typing required for the primary flow.

Append-only ledger

Immutable hash chain. No entry can be altered after confirmation. New records only, always.

Trust tiers

Behaviour earns authority. Not paperwork. Consistent recording over time builds a verifiable track record.

Capital routing

Verified history becomes collateral for credit. Structured exports support underwriting and reporting.

How it works

Say it. Confirm it. Keep it.

Step 01

Speak or type

"Sold rice for 15,000." Voice-first on Android and Chrome, text fallback everywhere else.

Step 02

Review the summary

The app reads it back in plain language so the user stays in control before anything becomes final.

Step 03

Confirm to write

Only explicit confirmation creates a ledger record. No edits, no deletes, no admin override.
