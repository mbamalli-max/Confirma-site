Plain-language product story

Not accounting software.
Record formation infrastructure.

Konfirmata is designed for people whose businesses are real, active, and valuable — but whose financial lives are still invisible to formal lenders.

For entrepreneurs

Freelancers, traders, service providers, and small business owners can build usable financial history from daily activity they already understand — no accounting background required.

For institutions

Banks and lenders can review transaction history that is structured, tamper-resistant, and easier to verify than spreadsheets or memory-based summaries.

For trust

Every confirmed transaction becomes permanent, receives a unique ID, and links back to the previous record through a cryptographic hash chain.

How it works

Say it. Confirm it. Keep it.

Speak or type a transaction

"Paid supplier 45,000" or "Paid vendor 1,200." Konfirmata supports voice-first transaction recording with text fallback.

Review the plain-language summary

The app reads it back so the user stays in control before anything becomes final.

Confirm to write permanently

Only explicit confirmation creates a ledger record. No edits, no deletes, no admin override path.

Why it matters

Your activity is already a credit story.

Konfirmata helps record that story in a way lenders can take seriously. Over time, each confirmed entry contributes to a persistent financial trail that can support underwriting, reporting, and trust.

  • Trust tiers move from NEW → BRONZE → SILVER → GOLD based on sustained activity.
  • Free exports include the ledger root hash, and verified exports can include a server attestation link and QR verification.
  • Active in Nigeria and the United States, with country-aware onboarding, labels, and currency formatting built in.
Trust tiers

Consistency becomes signal.

Konfirmata rewards continuity, not hype. The more consistently a business records real activity, the stronger its financial identity becomes.

NEW Starting the record

Every ledger begins with first use. The goal is simple: create a clear, trustworthy habit of confirmation-based recording.

BRONZE — 30 days Early continuity

After a month of active history, the ledger starts showing usable persistence and a foundation for first-level review.

SILVER — 90 days Bank-relevant history

Ninety days starts to resemble a meaningful operating picture. Reporting and underwriting conversations become stronger.

GOLD — 180 days Established financial identity

Six months of consistent, append-only activity creates a deeper record institutions can inspect with confidence.

"Your business activity is your credit history — if it's recorded right." Konfirmata is built around that idea from the first transaction to the exported report.
