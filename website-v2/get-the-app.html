Get the app

The app is a PWA — install from your browser, no app store needed

Konfirmata is delivered as a Progressive Web App. Open it in your mobile browser, add it to your home screen, and use it like an installed app. After setup, it is designed to keep working in low-connectivity environments.

Install steps

Open Konfirmata on your phone, then add it to your home screen.

Android

  • Open `/app` in Chrome on your Android phone
  • Accept the install prompt if Chrome shows one automatically
  • If you do not see the prompt, open the browser menu and choose the install option
  • Launch Konfirmata from your home screen like a normal app

iPhone

  • Open `/app` in Safari on your iPhone
  • Tap the Share button
  • Choose “Add to Home Screen”
  • Launch Konfirmata from your home screen like a normal app

FAQ

Practical answers before you install.

Can I use Konfirmata offline?

Yes. Konfirmata is designed as an offline-first PWA so entrepreneurs can continue recording in low-connectivity environments after setup.

What about data privacy?

The record belongs to the user. Institutions and partners only see exports the entrepreneur chooses to share.

Can I export my history later?

Yes. The export flow is how entrepreneurs package their recorded history for lender, program, audit, or partner review.

The install flow is intentionally simple: open the site, add it to your home screen, and use the app where the work is actually happening.
