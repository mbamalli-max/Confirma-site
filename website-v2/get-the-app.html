Install from the browser

Open Konfirmata on your phone and add it to your home screen.

Konfirmata is delivered as a Progressive Web App — it works on Android and iPhone without going through an app store. After installation, it keeps working offline.

Android / Chrome

Open the app and install when prompted.

Visit the live Konfirmata URL in Chrome on Android. Chrome should show an install prompt automatically, or you can use the browser menu to install.

Open Android app link
  • Open the production URL in Chrome
  • Tap the install prompt if it appears
  • If needed, use the Chrome menu and choose "Install app"
  • Launch Konfirmata from the home screen after installation
iPhone / Safari

Use "Add to Home Screen."

Open the production URL in Safari. Konfirmata can show guidance telling users to tap the Share button and then choose "Add to Home Screen."

Open iPhone app link
  • Open the production URL in Safari
  • Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
  • Select "Add to Home Screen"
  • Launch Konfirmata like a regular app from the home screen

Works offline

Konfirmata is designed so users can keep recording even when connectivity is weak or unavailable. Signed records sync when connection returns.

Designed for global use

Konfirmata supports Nigeria and the United States today, with country-aware onboarding, business types, and currency formatting.
