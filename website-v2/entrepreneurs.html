Why it matters

Your business may already be creditworthy. It just needs better records.

Many informal businesses generate steady activity every day, but that activity disappears because it was never captured in a bank-trusted format.

Speak naturally

Say "Sold 3 items for $120" or type it manually. Konfirmata is built to match the language of real work, not accounting forms.

Confirm before writing

No transaction becomes permanent unless you explicitly confirm it. That protects your authority over the record.

Grow a visible history

As you keep recording, Konfirmata shows streaks, trends, and trust tiers that make your business history easier to understand and present to lenders.

Designed for real business settings

Built for constrained environments, not office desks.

  • Works as a Progressive Web App on Android and iPhone
  • Installs to the home screen without going through an app store
  • Works offline after setup, and signed records sync when connection returns
  • Supports Nigeria and the United States today
  • Uses country-aware onboarding, business types, labels, and currency formatting
